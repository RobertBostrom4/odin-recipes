Miso Ramen

Description

This is a Miso Ramen recipe that combines steak, broth, miso paste, garlic, sesame oil, and soy sauce to create a wonderful bowl of noodles.

  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic

  • 2 (3 ounce) packages ramen noodles
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
