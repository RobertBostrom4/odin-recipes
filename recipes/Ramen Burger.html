Ramen Burger

Description

Instant ramen becomes a nearly-instant burger bun in this fun, and tasty, spin on a traditional hamburger seasoned with soy sauce and sesame oil.

Ingredients

  1. 2 (3 ounce) packages instant ramen noodles, flavor packet discarded
  2. 2 large eggs
  3. salt and ground black pepper to taste
  4. ¾ pound lean ground beef
  5. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  6. 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  7. 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  8. 3 slices American cheese
  9. ¼ cup ketchup
  10. 2 tablespoons chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha)
  11. 1 ½ cups arugula
  12. 3 large eggs
